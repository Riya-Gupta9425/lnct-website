The Lakshmi Narain College of Technology, Bhopal, is an educational institute based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. The private institute has a total of 2970 seats and offers 12 courses in different specializations. Across the 12 specializations, the courses offered include Chemical Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics Engineering, Civil Engineering, and more.

The placement ecosystem is also decent with the median salary being around ₹4,81,478. The institute has been recognized by leading accreditation bodies like AICTE.
